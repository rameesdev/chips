Our Story

Started as a small family kitchen in 19xx, **CM Palahara Kada** has grown into a beloved destination for authentic Kerala snacks. Our secret lies in using locally sourced Nendran bananas and 100% pure coconut oil, ensuring every bite takes you back to your childhood.

Every snack is handmade daily, following time-honored recipes passed down through generations. We don't just sell snacks; we share the rich tradition of Kerala.
